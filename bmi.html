“Xplore Lanka” is a simple to use, Sri Lankan based Journey planning application, where you can workout on your journey plan based on the weather and day you want to visit a particular place in Sri Lanka.

We use machine learning to predict the future weather to a certain location that is not an option in other travel applications out there.
